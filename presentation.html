Технологи

Frontend

• Vanilla JS (PWA-тай)
• MathLive — math keyboard
• KaTeX — math rendering
• jsQR, qrcodejs — QR scanning
• jsPDF + html2canvas — PDF

Backend

• Vercel Serverless Functions
• Node.js (pg + bcrypt + jwt)
• Neon PostgreSQL (50GB plan)
• Google OAuth 2.0
• Хүчирхэг middleware

Хадгалалт

• PostgreSQL (12+ хүснэгт)
• localStorage (offline support)
• Vercel Blob (image upload)
• Email via Resend
